What this generates

Based on the client profile and interview, the engine populates all production IMS templates:

✓ Scope Statement — client name, sites, activities
✓ PESTLE Analysis — sector-specific factors and UK legislation
✓ Risk Register — PESTLE + SWOT + operational risks
✓ 9 IMS Procedures — all populated with client data
✓ Objectives Planner — SMART targets with baselines
✓ Legal Compliance Register — sector-relevant laws
✓ Environmental Aspects Register — process-specific aspects
✓ Process Interaction Map — client process names
Consultant review built in

Every generated document goes through a mandatory 12-point consultant review checklist before it can be exported and released to the client.

The sign-off workflow records the reviewer name, date and approval decision on each document.
